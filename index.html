Questions?

We've got you.

What types of pets do you care for?

We care for dogs and cats primarily, but also have experience with small mammals like rabbits and guinea pigs.

Are vaccinations required before boarding?

Yes, we require up-to-date vaccination records for all boarding pets to ensure the health and safety of all our guests.

Can I bring my pet's own food or bedding?

Absolutely! We encourage bringing familiar items to help your pet feel more at home during their stay.

How do you handle emergencies?

We have a veterinarian on call 24/7 and will immediately contact you in case of any emergency.

What are your operating hours?

Our kennel is open for drop-off and pick-up from 8am to 6pm, 7 days a week. Staff are on-site 24/7 to care for your pets.
